<h2>Middle and High School Awards</h2>

<p>The Howard County Public School System's annual Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics Fair &amp; Festival was held on March 1, 2014 at Long Reach High School, and was attended by more than 1,000 people!  The Festival featured more than 150 interactive displays exhibits from students and schools.</p>
