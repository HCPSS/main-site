Open contract negotiation meetings between representatives of Howard County Education Association and HCPSS staff will take place at 4 p.m. at The Ascend One Building, 8930 Stanford Blvd., Columbia, MD., on the dates listed below. All meetings are open to the public.

11/26/13     4 p.m. Chesapeake Room Ascend One
12/12/13     4 p.m Susquehanna Room Ascend One
12/19/13     4 p.m. Chesapeake Room Ascend One

For minutes of previous meetings please visit: <a href="http://www.boarddocs.com/mabe/hcpssmd/Board.nsf/goto?open&amp;id=9CZQEN62930B" target="_blank">http://www.boarddocs.com/mabe/hcpssmd/Board.nsf/goto?open&amp;id=9CZQEN62930B</a>

Individuals who would like to request a sign language interpreter for any meeting of the Board of Education may contact Colleen Rice,  Interpreter Scheduler, at 410-313-7046.  A two week advanced notice is preferred.
