The HCPSS invites middle and high school students to share input about how bullying behaviors affect the school environment at any of four evening forums. Student leaders will facilitate discussion in small groups. The information gathered will inform future recommendations from the Anti-Bullying Task Force.

A separate waiting area for parents will be available during each session. Students should <a href="http://tinyurl.com/hcpssstudentforum" target="_blank">register</a> for only one forum as all sessions are the same. Space is limited so please register by March 5, 2013.
